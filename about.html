What To Do

Located on the foot of beautiful lake Vättern, Jönköping is a wonderful city with a lot to do. Options include:

  • Tändsticksmuseet
  • Jönköpings Läns Museum
  • Jönköpings Stadspark

Going Out?

If you're a student, i can't recommend Akademien enough. Akademien is the offical student club of the university and a great place to hang out with new and old friends alike. If you're going out on a weekend however, Akademien isn't open. In that case I would recommend the following bars:

  • Shooters
  • Hörnan
  • Kappa Bar
  • Pitchers
